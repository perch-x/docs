Welcome to Aongtong Homestay

Aongtong Homestay is located in a central part of Rong Kwang town far from busy roads. Still it's then less 1 km from an intercity bus stop. The house is made of wood, very cosy and equipped for a long stayings.
There are two private rooms with queen beds.  Some extra sleeping spaces are available in the living room for those who travel by a small group.

There is hot shower, free Wi Fi , flat-screen TV, laptop friendly workspace. Kitchen and dining room are inside the house. The kitchen has all nessesary tableware and cooking utensil including espresso machine, electric kettle and grill. The backyarde is equipped for barbeque parties. Right behind the house we’ve just started a small organic farm and we would like to share our vegetables with customers. Every morning we provide breakfast. We’d like to share our culture with guests and cook traditional food of Northern Thailand. Customers can try menu of Lan Ban Kik Cafe as well.
We provide bycicles and free tours around Rong Kwang for our customers. Tours around Phrae province are avalable for extra fee.
